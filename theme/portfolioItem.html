LANDING PAGE

Idea

Develop a stylish Landing for a company specializing in recruiting in the field of telecommunications and information technology.

Tasks

  1. Create a stylish landing page for a company specializing in recruiting in the field of telecommunications and information technology
  2. Create a stylish landing page for a company specializing in recruiting in the field of telecommunications and information technology
  3. Create a stylish landing page for a company specializing in recruiting in the field of telecommunications and information technology

Result

As a result of the development, we presented a bright and original landing page, which fully meets the requirements of the customer: laconic, informative and creative. Most of the content is presented in a visual form (geography of work, report on the work done for the last year), which facilitates perception of information. Animated icons attract attention that emphasizes the main colors of the layout. In the middle is a catalog of vacancies, which has a block for requirements, responsibilities and photos. For the global search, two language versions - Russian and English - have been implemented, which will allow them to find work for applicants outside Ukraine. The window for communication allows not only to leave contact information, but also to attach a resume to accelerate employment.
